About Jori Haka

With over ten years of hands-on experience in construction, carpentry, and home repairs, Jori Haka is a dedicated professional committed to transforming spaces with quality and precision. From small repairs to full renovations, Jori provides reliable solutions that ensure the durability and functionality of every project.

Expert in maintenance, finishing, and remodeling, he works with great attention to detail and adaptability, responding to the specific needs of each client. His approach combines traditional techniques with modern methods to deliver efficient and long-lasting results.

With experience in Finland and Malta, and fluent communication in Finnish, English, and Spanish, Jori is the ideal partner for any construction or repair work in your home.
